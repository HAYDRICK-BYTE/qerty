About Us

OBJECTIVE

Our main focus is to help the youth better themselves in various aspects (educational, financial, after-schooling life, emotional & physical health) of their lives.

GOALS

  • Participants must be able to identify learning patterns.
  • The participants of this program should be able to generate their own budget plan, manage and save money.
  • At the end of this program, participant should gain some skills through the goal-setting sessions, peer conflict coaching & public speaking practice.

Every kid finds their way faster with someone in their corner.

This program seeks to help kids navigate their way in life, one-on-one support — with schoolwork, with basic financial skills, with confidence, and with figuring out what's next.
